I'm a researcher focusing especially on the field of movement ecology of wild animals. I use innovative dataloggers and state-of-the-art statistical methods to track wildlife to investigate their habitat selection and behavioural activities. I wish to improve our understanding of how animals behave, use, and move in the landscape, which is needed to elucidate the ecological causes, mechanisms, and spatiotemporal patterns on how wildlife interact, survive, and thrive in a changing world. Both on individual-, population-, and community-level.
