I'm a researcher in movement ecology, focusing especially on the natural behaviour and spatial dynamics of wild animals. I use innovative dataloggers and state-of-the-art statistical methods to track wildlife and investigate their habitat selection and behavioural activities under various ecological conditions. I wish to improve our understanding of how animals move, behave, and use landscapes, which is needed to elucidate the ecological causes, mechanisms, and spatiotemporal patterns that shape how wildlife interact, survive, and thrive in a changing world - across individual, population, and community levels.
